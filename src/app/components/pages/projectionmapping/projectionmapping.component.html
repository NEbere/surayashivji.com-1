Electroland

Role: Animation, Design

Tools: AfterEffects, Three.js

Projection Mapping Experiment

Overview

Electroland is a projection mapping experiment created by myself (Animation, Design), Eddie Mestre (Music Production, Projection Mapping, Animation), and Jamie Haberman (Animation, Design). This project visually explores the evolution of electricity, taking the audience on a journey into the future of technology. Our team storyboarded each scene, designed and animated 2D assets, and then mapped these assets onto a 3D form built from foam core.
