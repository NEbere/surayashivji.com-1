Overview

Mello is a startup that I cofounded with my roommate Jamie Haberman. We grew our team to 8 in order to fully strategize, research, design, develop, and build Mello. Though aren't working on Mello full-time currently, I've never learned more about entrepreneurship, management, design, and tech than I did while iterating and pivoting on this product for a year.

Meet mello. The first smart fragrance product for young adults that intelligently selects scents for your varying needs using organic essential oils. Simply push your monthly cartridge of oils into the Mello diffuser, and it will fill your room with a scent that matches each moment based on your schedule, goals, and data from connected devices.

While current home products focus on technological and voice innovation, our team believed that the home should care for you. Home products should be approached from a point of wellness, not just technology. So, our vision for Mello was a suite of products for the home that focus on personalized, data-driven, and passive wellness.
