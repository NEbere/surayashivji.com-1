Overview

Mello is a project I’m working on with my roommate Jamie Haberman. Starting out as a team of two, we have been able to have our hands in the market & user research, design, tech, and business aspects of this ongoing project. Our current team consists of Jamie and myself leading design and software, Eric Deng leading hardware, Caitlyn Clabaugh leading machine learning, and Colleen Quinn leading aromatherapy R&D.

Mello is an intelligent diffuser for the home that motivates a lifestyle of wellness for young adults through personalized aromatherapy. Mello leverages artificial intelligence to mechanically create blends in real time based on the user’s changing moods and stress factors.
