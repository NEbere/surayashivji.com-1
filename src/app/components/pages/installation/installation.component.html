"The Journey" Installation

Overview

The Journey is an installation created by myself, Jamie Haberman (Animation, Editing), and Eddie Mestre (Animation, Editing, Production). This project was installed at USC's annual music festival, Springfest, which brought together thousands of students and featured performances from popular musical artists such as Still Woozy, Gunna, JID and more. Our project was selected to be a part of their immersive playground that was filled with interactive experiences and installations. In collaboration with Eastside LED, our team constructed 5 12ft video panels that we spaced out in a semi-circle formation for viewers to step into. The piece explores a variety of abstract landscapes and experiments created in Cinema4D and with Three.js which we composited together in After Effects to music by Kaytranada.
