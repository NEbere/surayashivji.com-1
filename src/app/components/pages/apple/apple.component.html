Live Listen

Live listens turns your iOS device into a directional microphone by seeding the sound to your AirPods. By placing your iOS device closer to a sound source, you will be better able to listen to a conversation in a noisy environment or even hear someone speaking across the room. Though Live Listen is not a full replacement for hearing aids, there are 48 million adults in the US who report hearing loss, but only 26% actually own hearing aids (partly because of cost). This feature is an affordable and accessible solution to a growing problem.
